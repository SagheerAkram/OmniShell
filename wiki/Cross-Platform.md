| OS | Supported | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Linux** | ✅ | Validated on Ubuntu, Debian, Arch, Fedora. |
| **Windows** | ✅ | Windows 10/11 x64. Requires MSVC. |
| **macOS** | ✅ | Intel and Apple Silicon (M1/M2). |
| **Android** | 🚧 | Termux support planned. |
| **iOS** | ❌ | No current plans. |
